Wateryam Balls

Hi guys! Today, i will be sharing with you my recipe for wateryam balls, popularly known as "ojojo". I love wateryam balls so much, and in my opinion, it is too underrated.

Ingredients:

  • Wateryam
  • Cooking oil
  • Bell pepper
  • Spring onions
  • Salt
  • Seasoning cube (Optional)

Preparation Steps:

Step 1: Cut your wateryam into chunks, wash wih water and grate them.

Step 2: Chop your bell peppers (as much as you prefer) and spring onions.

Step 3: Mix your chopped vegetables together with your grated wateryam. Add seasonings.

Step 4: Put your pan on your stove, pour enough oil and heat it for a minute or two.

Step 5: Scoop your wateryam mixture into the hot oil. Fry until golden brown.

Tips and Variations:

Tip 1: Wateryam balls should be fried on low heat and should not stay too long on the fire.

Tip 2: It is best you dissolve the seasonings in water before adding to the wateryam mixture.

Variation: You may decide to add prawns, crayfish, etc, for more flavour.
